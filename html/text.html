you cannot dream in the bleakness


02:00 GMT September 2

don’t dismiss the darkness, invite it to inspire creativity and passion. the darkness is a gateway to a world of possibilities. nothing is good or bad, those are made up concepts sounds like star wars shit haha.. accept the dark side’ , but you really should, don’t dismiss the occasional ‘terrible’ thought, instead see it through. people think they’re better than who they are. in reality people are assholes who live on this planet with no existential purpose worth living for, so we substitute that need with morals, values, and relationships. Dismissing dark thoughts is like dismissing a part of yourself, you should see it through, you aren’t better than the person you see in your thoughts, you are the person in your thoughts. trying to avoid ‘bad’ thoughts is childish and stupid, and leaves you half blind to the truth of it all.
